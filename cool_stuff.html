Things that seem cool to me

One of my hopes for my blog is that it will be a useful place to collect and organize interesting reference images or threads of inquiry.

I am interested in letters, fonts, and typography. I like letters on the edge of legibility, heavily flourished ornamental forms. I'm interested in letters made at the turn of the 20th century and around the new millenium. I like connected scripts and type that immitates handwriting, and I will always have the utmost reverence for hand lettered forms.
